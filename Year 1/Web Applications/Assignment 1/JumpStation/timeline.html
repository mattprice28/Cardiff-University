Search Engine Timeline

JumpStation was the building block for all modern search engines that followed

Search Engine Year Introduced
JumpStation 1993
WebCrawler* 1994
Yahoo! 1995
Ask Jeeves 1996
Google 1998
MSN Search 1998
AOL Search 2005
Bing** 2009

*WebCrawler is the oldest search engine still running

**Bing is a rebrand of MSN Search

Jonathon Fletcher was not made aware of his contribution to the internet until 2013, 20 years after JumpStation was first introduced and long after he ceased to maintain it. Fletcher was invited as a guest speaker to a conference in Dublin celebrating the 15th Birthday of Google.
